About Maddie

Madison "Maddie P." Petaway is a performance poet from Houston, TX. She was on the youth slam team Meta-4-Houston and during her time became the first Black female youth poet laureate of Houston, TX. Some other noteworthy accomplishments include publication of her piece "To Be a Black Girl" in the New York Times and being a speaker for 2023 TEDxUTAustin.
